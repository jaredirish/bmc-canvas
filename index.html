Business Model Canvas: Apple Inc.

Key Partners

  • TSMC - how they make every Apple chip
  • Foxconn - inside the iPhone factory
  • App developers - 36M+ registered, ecosystem value
  • Carriers - AT&T, Verizon, T-Mobile (distribution + subsidy)
  • Content providers - music labels, studios, publishers
  • Enterprise - IBM, SAP, Salesforce integrations

Key Activities

  • Hardware design - Jony Ive's design philosophy
  • Software/OS - iOS, macOS, watchOS, visionOS
  • Chip design - how Apple Silicon changed everything
  • Retail operations - 500+ Apple Stores globally
  • Services growth - Apple TV+, Music, Fitness+, iCloud
  • Privacy as product - Apple's privacy war explained

Key Resources

  • Brand - #1 most valuable brand globally
  • Silicon team - custom chip design (ex-ARM)
  • iOS ecosystem - 2B+ active devices, lock-in
  • Cash - $160B+ cash reserves
  • Retail footprint - highest revenue/sq ft in retail
  • Supply chain - Tim Cook's operational mastery

Value Propositions

  • Integrated ecosystem - hardware + software + services
  • Premium design - industrial design as moat
  • Privacy - on-device ML, ATT, passkeys
  • Status/identity - blue vs green bubble explained
  • Longevity - 5-7 year software support
  • It just works - lower friction
  • Developer platform - App Store for 36M devs

Customer Relationships

  • Genius Bar - in-person support
  • AppleCare - warranty as recurring revenue
  • Ecosystem lock-in - switching cost per device
  • Today at Apple - free creative sessions
  • Brand community - identity-level loyalty

Channels

  • Apple Stores - 500+ flagship retail
  • apple.com - direct online
  • Carrier partners - subsidized sales
  • Best Buy / retailers - third-party
  • Enterprise direct - Apple Business Manager
  • App Store - digital distribution

Customer Segments

  • Mass consumer - iPhone, iPad, AirPods
  • Creative professionals - Mac, Pro apps
  • Enterprise/education - fleet, MDM
  • Developers - Xcode, App Store
  • Health/fitness - Watch, Health app
  • Spatial computing - Vision Pro

Cost Structure

  • R&D: $30B+/year - silicon, software
  • Manufacturing - COGS ~60% hardware
  • Retail ops - 500+ stores
  • Content - TV+, Music licensing
  • Marketing - premium campaigns
  • Legal - antitrust, patents

Revenue Streams

  • iPhone: ~52% - $200B+
  • Services: ~25% - $95B+ (70%+ margin)
  • Mac: ~8%
  • iPad: ~6%
  • Wearables: ~9%
  • Hardware margin: 36%
